Burger

Description

A hamburger, or simply a burger, is a dish consisting of fillings—usually a patty of ground meat, typically beef—placed inside a sliced bun or bread roll.

Ingredients

  • Ground Chuck Beef
  • Potato Buns
  • Sliced cheese
  • Dill pickle slices
  • Fresh red onions (or caramelized onions)
  • Tomato
  • Green leaf or iceberg lettuce
  • Avocado
  • Bacon + pineapple + the BBQ sauce below
  • Jalapeños to add some heat
    • Sauce
  • Mayonnaise - 1/3 cup
  • BBQ sauce - 1tbsp

Instructions

  1. Shape the patties 1” wider than the bun since they shrink on the grill, don't overwork your meat - this will make it tough and dense.
  2. Make an indentation in the center of the patty to prevent it from plumping up in the center.
  3. Get a good Sear - Once on the grill, let patties brown and sear well (3-5 min) before flipping
  4. Sauce - Apply a generous amount of sauce to both buns.
  5. Toppings - Place pickles, lettuce, tomatoes, and red onion onto the bottom bun
  6. Patty - Place cheesy burger patty over the veggies and cover with top bun
